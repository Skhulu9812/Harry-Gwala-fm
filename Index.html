About Us

Harry Gwala FM is the first community radio station in Umzimkhulu and the only district in KwaZulu-Natal that previously had no community radio. We are dedicated to serving the people of Harry Gwala District with informative, educational, and entertaining content.

Our Mission

To be the voice of the community, promoting local culture, providing information, and fostering development through quality radio programming.

Our Vision

To be the leading community radio station in KwaZulu-Natal, recognized for excellence in broadcasting and community engagement.

Our Values

Integrity, Community, Diversity, Innovation, and Excellence in everything we do.
